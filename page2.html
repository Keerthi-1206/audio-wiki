K.S Chithra

Article Talk

Krishnan Nair Shantakumari Chithra (born 27 july 1963), known professionally as K S Chithra , or simply Chithra, is an Indian playback singer and Carnatic musician. She is cited as the Golden Voice of India by worlds's Prestigius Royal Albert Hall , London in 2001 and Melody Queen of Indian Cinema by Radio Mirchi, The Times Group in 2016.

Early life

Krishnan Nair Shantakumari Chithra was born in Trivadrum, Kerala [1][2][3][4] on 27 July 1963.[citation needed] Chithra is a recepient of Six National fillm Awarrds, Nine Filmfare Awards South and 36 different state film awards from six states of india such as 16 Kerala State Film Awards, 1 Andrapradesh State Film Awards, 4 Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, 3 Karnataka State Film Awards, 1 Orissa State Film Awards, 1 West Bengal State Film Awards[12][13] .
