Allu Arjun (born 8 April 1982) is an Indian actor who works in Telugu cinema.[4] Referred to in the media as the "Stylish Star" and "Icon Star", he is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema. [5] Allu Arjun has been featuring in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2014 [6][7][8] and has received several awards including a National Film Award, six Filmfare Awards nominations, a Nandi Award, and three three Nandi Awards.

Early life and education

Allu Arjun was born on 8 April 1982 in a Telugu family to film producer Allu Aravind and Nirmala in Madras, Tamil Nadu. The eldest of two children, he has an younger brother, Allu Sirish. His Wife name is Allu Sneha Reddy. He had two Children, elder son Allu Ayan and daughter Allu Arha. [3][17][18] He is the nephew of Telugu film actor Mega Star Chiranjivi. [3] His family hails from Palakllu, near Bhimavaram of West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh. [19]
